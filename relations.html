How the relation works with different countries?

Geopolitics is responsible for good or bad relations between nations.

The Geo-Political Relation between India and Russia

India and Russia have cordial relations.
The two countries have a strategic partnership signed in 2000,
and India considers Russia one of its closest allies.

The Geo-Political Relation between India and China

The relationship between India and China has soured over the last decade.
The 2020 border brawl between Indian and Chinese troops in the Galwan Valley is one reason for this.

The Geo-Political Relation between India and USA

The relationship between the United States and India is one of the most strategic and consequential of the 21st century.
The United States supports India's emergence as a leading global power and a vital partner in promoting a peaceful, stable, and prosperous Indo-Pacific region.
